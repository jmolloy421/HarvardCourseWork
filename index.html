The Wombats

The White Plains Wombats are an American Rugby League club based out of White Plains, New York.

Established in 2016, we compete in the USA Rugby League(USARL) competition in the Northern Region.

The Wombats are sister clubs with the White Plains Rugby Union Club. The two teams form A 501c(3) Organization Dedicated to the Development of the Sport of Rugby Football, the Personal Growth of Young Men and Women, and Support of Our Local Community.
